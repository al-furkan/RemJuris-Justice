Mohammad Ikbal Hasan

Advocate
Supreme Court of Bangladesh

Mohammad Ikbal Hasan is a preeminent Advocate of the Supreme Court of Bangladesh, known for his expertise in Civil,Criminal, and Writ law, with a specialization in financial and corporate law. With over a decade of legal practice, he has a notable record of success in litigation, advisory roles, and strategic legal solutions for the financial sector.

Hasan completed his Bachelor of Laws with Honours (LLB Hons) in 2008 and Master of Laws (LLM) in 2009 at the University of Chittagong, Bangladesh. He further earned an M Phil in Law in 2017 and is currently a PhD Research Fellow studying the protection of Bangladeshi domestic workers.

He was granted permission to practice in the High Court Division of the Supreme Court of Bangladesh on 19 August 2013, following his enrollment as an Advocate of the Bangladesh Bar Council on 08 December 2009. He is a member of the Dhaka Taxes Bar Association.

In academia, Hasan served as the Assistant Professor and Head of the Department of Law at the University of Information, Technology, and Sciences (UITS) in Dhaka. He is the author of "A Textbook on Land Law" (2015) and numerous scholarly articles. His contributions to legal education and his advocacy for the third gender population in Bangladesh earned him the 'Lawyer’s Fellowship' by USAID in 2017.

Hasan's career reflects his dedication to legal excellence, education, and the pursuit of justice, making him a respected figure in Bangladesh’s legal community.
